Form vs. Server: The Key Difference

Front-end validation (like the required and pattern attributes above) runs in the browser, instantly, before anything is sent — it improves the user experience but can be bypassed. Server-side handling happens after submission: the server (using PHP, Node.js, etc.) must independently re-validate and process the data, because client-side checks alone are never secure enough to trust on their own.
