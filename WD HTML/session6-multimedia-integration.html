Session 6 — Multimedia Integration

Embedding Video: <video>

The <video> tag embeds video directly, with attributes to control playback:

<video controls width="500">
  <source src="intro.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  Your browser does not support video.
</video>

controls shows play/pause/volume UI · autoplay starts playback automatically · loop replays the video when it ends.

Embedding Audio: <audio>

<audio controls>
  <source src="theme.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
  Your browser does not support audio.
</audio>

Audio uses the same core attributes as video: controls, autoplay, and loop. Autoplay with sound is blocked by most modern browsers unless the user has interacted with the page first.
